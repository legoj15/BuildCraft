<chapter name="Information"/>
The Creative Engine is only available in creative mode or with cheats.
It requires no fuel and never overheats.
<recipes_usages stack="buildcraftunofficial:engine_creative"/>

<chapter name="Output Levels"/>
Interact with the engine while holding a Wrench (without holding the crouch key) to cycle its output through 9 power levels:
<bold>1, 2, 4, 8, 16, 32, 64, 128, 256 MJ/t</bold>.

<chapter name="Engine Mechanics"/>
Engines will always connect to the nearest compatible MJ consumer.
You can use a Wrench while crouching to rotate the engine if it's facing at the wrong block.
As with all engines, it <bold>requires a redstone signal to run.</bold>
Gates can still be wired to its power-stage triggers, but since it never leaves Black, only the Black trigger will ever fire.
