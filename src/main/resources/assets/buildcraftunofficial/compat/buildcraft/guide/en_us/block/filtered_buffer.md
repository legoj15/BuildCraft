<lore>
A more refined intermediate than having a chest collect items for temporary storage, the Filtered Buffer will only accept items if they match one of the 9 configurable slots.
</lore>
<no_lore>
The Filtered Buffer only accepts items if they're defined in one of the 9 filter slots.
</no_lore>
<chapter name="Information"/>
The GUI is two rows of nine slots. The <bold>top row</bold> is where the filters are defined. An empty filter slot means nothing will be accepted for that slot. The <bold>bottom row</bold> are the buffer slots; a single slot of inventory space belonging to the filter above it. When a buffer slot is empty and a filter for that slot is defined, a preview of the valid item will be rendered. If both the filter slot and the buffer slot are empty, the slot becomes closed and will accept no more items.
