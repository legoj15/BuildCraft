<lore>
You've got your blueprint, but you wished you had used stone instead of cobble... instead of rebuilding and setting up the Architect Table again, just replace all the cobble within the blueprint itself!
</lore>
<no_lore>
The Replacer will take one type of block from a blueprint and replace it with another, modifying the blueprint directly.
</no_lore>
<chapter name="Information"/>
Insert a <link inline="buildcraftunofficial:item/blueprint_clean"/> with snapshot data into the top slot. Below, two <link inline="buildcraftunofficial:placeholder/schematic_single_clean"/> slots: the <bold>left</bold> is the block to find, the <bold>right</bold> is the block to substitute in. You may also rename the blueprint at this time. 
Replacement is instant and requires no power.

The 3D preview at the top rotates a live model of the Blueprint, showing you the result of the change before the replacement happens.
