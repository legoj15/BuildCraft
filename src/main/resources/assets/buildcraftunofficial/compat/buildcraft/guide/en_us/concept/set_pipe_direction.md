Pipes that have their functionality linked to what direction they're facing, like an Iron Pipe or a Wooden Pipe with multiple extraction sources, need a way to be told which side from a gate. The six "Face the X side" gate actions do that: pick one in the gate's Action slot and the pipe re-orients to that direction whenever the gate fires. 
The color coding of the names match that of the colored directional faces of the Diamond pipe.

A valid pipe with all 4 of its cardinal directions and top and bottom sides with valid connections will show all 6 directions as settable in the gate.
