Pipes that have their functionality linked to what direction they're facing, like an <link inline="buildcraftunofficial:pipe/iron_item"/> or a <link inline="buildcraftunofficial:pipe/wood_item"/> with multiple extraction sources, can have their working direction changed with a connected gate. This is achieved with up to 6 actions, one for each facing direction.
The color coding of the names match that of the colored directional faces of the Diamond pipe.
On pipes other than the <link inline="buildcraftunofficial:pipe/daizuli_item"/>, the possible pipe directions in the gate are limited to the pipe's current connections; if you have an Iron Pipe only inline with the East-West direction and no other connections, the only available directions will be East and West. A valid pipe with all 4 of its cardinal directions and top and bottom sides with valid connections will show all 6 directions as settable in the gate.
