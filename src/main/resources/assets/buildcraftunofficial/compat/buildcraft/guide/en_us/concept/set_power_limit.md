<lore>
The Iron and Diamond power-pipe variants throttle the energy passing through them. A connected gate can step the throttle up or down at will, swapping the pipe's transfer cap to a different rate.
</lore>
<no_lore>
"Set Power Limit" is a family of gate actions provided on any gate placed on an Iron or Diamond power pipe (both the MJ and RF variants). Each entry sets the pipe's transfer cap to a specific rate; selecting one is equivalent to right-clicking the pipe with a wrench until that rate is shown.
</no_lore>

<chapter name="Behaviour"/>
The MJ kinesis pipes (<link inline="buildcraftunofficial:placeholder/pipe_iron_power"/> and <link inline="buildcraftunofficial:placeholder/pipe_diamond_power"/>) expose seven MJ/t actions, from the pipe's full transfer rate down to 0 MJ/t. The FE/RF pipes (<link inline="buildcraftunofficial:placeholder/pipe_iron_rf"/> and <link inline="buildcraftunofficial:placeholder/pipe_diamond_rf"/>) expose the same seven steps in RF/t. Selecting the 0-rate entry blocks all power transfer through the pipe entirely.
