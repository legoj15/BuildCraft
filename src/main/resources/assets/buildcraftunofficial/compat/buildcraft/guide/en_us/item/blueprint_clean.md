<lore>
Your medium for capturing even the most minute of details of a structure. Slot one into an <link inline="buildcraftunofficial:block/architect"/> to capture everything it can see, then load it into a <link inline="buildcraftunofficial:block/builder"/> to manufacture an exact copy.
</lore>
<no_lore>
A blank Blueprint, ready to be filled by an <link inline="buildcraftunofficial:block/architect"/>. Stacks to 16. Once a captured area is scanned onto it, the result is a unique single-stack item carrying the full block-state of that area, which a <link inline="buildcraftunofficial:block/builder"/> can re-create.
</no_lore>
<chapter name="Information"/>
Insert a Blueprint into the input slot of an Architect Table bound to a Land Mark volume. When the scan finishes, the input blueprint is used (whether it is clean or already used) and is placed into the output slot with the new data written to it. The blueprint will show the name and a 3D snapshot of the captured structure in its tooltip.
To deploy the saved structure, slot the blueprint into a <link inline="buildcraftunofficial:block/builder"/> and supply it with the same resources that were used at the time of the blueprint's capture.
If only the general shape of a structure is needed, see <link inline="buildcraftunofficial:item/template_clean"/> for faster scanning and simpler building.
