<chapter name="Information"/>
A creative-only debug item that can be placed on the corner of a <link inline="buildcraftunofficial:item/box_volume"/> and activated by interacting with it while a <link inline="buildcraftunofficial:item/marker_connector"/> is in hand.
Can only be placed in a single corner of a <bold>Volume Box</bold>. A <link inline="buildcraftunofficial:block/filler"/> is not required. Open the GUI to select between all of the available patterns the Filler has, and the target shape will be render as translucent cubes. To remove from a Volume Box, crouch while interacting with a Marker Connector in hand.
You can resize the Volume Box and the Filler Planner will adapt to the shape.
