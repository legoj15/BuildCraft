</lore>
<no_lore>
A Fragile Fluid Shard is an itemized form of a fluid only available via breaking a block that contains fluid.
</no_lore>
<chapter name="Information"/>
Fragile fluid shards are a form of itemized fluid storage. Breaking a BuildCraft block that contains fluid will drop all of their contained fluids as fluid shards, divided into 500 mB items. Fluid shards can be inserted back into tanks or machines like you would with a bucket. Once a fluid shard is completely empty, it is discarded. Fluid shards cannot be refilled.
