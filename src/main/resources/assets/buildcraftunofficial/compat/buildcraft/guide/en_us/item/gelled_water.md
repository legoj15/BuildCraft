<lore>
You need some water gone? It's easier to handle when it's a solid.
</lore>
<no_lore>
Gelled Water is an itemized form of water that is easier to store and makes clearing out small volumes of water trivial.
</no_lore>
<chapter name="Information"/>
Obtained by using a <bold>Water Gellifier</bold> on a water source, Gelled Water is obtained by waiting for the water to reach its final solid form. One gellifier will affect multiple water sources, so bulk clearing/creation of gelled water is possible. Water will not solidify into its final form if a water source is touching it, so the borders of a chunk of gelled water will stay somewhat wet.
