<chapter name="Pipe Mechanics"/>
The Cobblestone Kinesis Pipe is the cheapest power conduit. It carries up to 4 MJ/t by default and loses the most energy of any Kinesis Pipe over distance, so it is best kept to short, low-power runs.

Power is fed into the network by a Wooden Kinesis Pipe and delivered to any adjacent machine that consumes MJ.
<link to="buildcraftunofficial:pipe/wood_power"/>

Cobblestone Kinesis Pipes will not connect to Stone or Quartz Kinesis Pipes.
Pipe Plugs, or painting pipes a different colour, can also stop pipes connecting.
