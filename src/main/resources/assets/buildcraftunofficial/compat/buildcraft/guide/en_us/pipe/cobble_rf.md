<chapter name="Pipe Mechanics"/>
The Cobblestone FE Pipe is the cheapest conduit, carrying up to 40 FE/t by default - the lowest rate of any FE Pipe - so it is best kept to low-throughput runs.

Forge Energy is fed into the network by a Wooden FE Pipe and delivered to any adjacent machine that consumes FE.
<link to="buildcraftunofficial:pipe/wood_rf"/>

Cobblestone FE Pipes will not connect to Stone or Quartz FE Pipes.
Pipe Plugs, or painting pipes a different colour, can also stop pipes connecting.
