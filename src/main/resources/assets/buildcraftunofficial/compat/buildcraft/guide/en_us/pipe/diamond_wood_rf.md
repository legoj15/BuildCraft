<chapter name="Pipe Mechanics"/>
The Wooden Diamond FE Pipe works just like the Wooden FE Pipe - it is the input that draws Forge Energy out of a source and hands it to the network - but carries far more, up to 2,560 FE/t by default.
<link to="buildcraftunofficial:pipe/wood_rf"/>

Forge Energy has no item types to filter, so the diamond simply raises the transfer rate over the Wooden FE Pipe. It will not connect to another wooden FE pipe.

<chapter name="Powering"/>
An MJ Dynamo or any other Forge Energy source can feed it.
<link to="buildcraftunofficial:block/mj_dynamo"/>
