<chapter name="Pipe Mechanics"/>
The Golden Kinesis Pipe is the high-throughput power conduit, carrying up to 128 MJ/t by default. Use it for the trunk lines that feed your most demanding machines.

Power is fed into the network by a Wooden Kinesis Pipe and delivered to any adjacent machine that consumes MJ.
<link to="buildcraftunofficial:pipe/wood_power"/>

Pipe Plugs, or painting pipes a different colour, can stop pipes connecting.
