<chapter name="Pipe Mechanics"/>
The Golden FE Pipe is the high-throughput conduit, carrying up to 1,280 FE/t by default. Use it for the trunk lines that feed your most demanding machines.

Forge Energy is fed into the network by a Wooden FE Pipe and delivered to any adjacent machine that consumes FE.
<link to="buildcraftunofficial:pipe/wood_rf"/>

Pipe Plugs, or painting pipes a different colour, can stop pipes connecting.
