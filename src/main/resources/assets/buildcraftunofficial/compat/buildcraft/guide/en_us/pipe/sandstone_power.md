<chapter name="Pipe Mechanics"/>
The Sandstone Kinesis Pipe carries up to 16 MJ/t by default. Its special trait is that it connects only to other pipes - never to a machine, engine, or other block beside it.

This lets a power line run right past a block you do not want to feed, where any other Kinesis Pipe would deliver power into it.

Power is fed into the network by a Wooden Kinesis Pipe.
<link to="buildcraftunofficial:pipe/wood_power"/>
Pipe Plugs, or painting pipes a different colour, can also stop pipes connecting.
