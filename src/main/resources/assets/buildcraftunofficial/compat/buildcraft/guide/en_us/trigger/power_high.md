<lore>
Is your machine's power buffer about to overflow? This is your warning.
</lore>
<no_lore>
High Power Stored is a gate trigger provided on any gate adjacent to a machine that holds an MJ buffer.
</no_lore>

<chapter name="Requirements"/>
When selected, the connected actions will activate while the adjacent machine's stored power is at or above 95% of its capacity. Use it to throttle engines that are filling a battery faster than the machine can drain it, or to redirect spillover power to a different consumer.

<chapter name="Trigger Directions"/>
Gates may show the High Power Stored trigger multiple times in different colours to represent each of the different adjacent machines. The colour of the trigger represents the direction of the machine in relation to the gate so you can differentiate between each machine.
