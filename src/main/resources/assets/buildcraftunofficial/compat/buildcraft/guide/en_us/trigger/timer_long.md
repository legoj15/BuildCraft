<no_lore>
15 Sec Timer is a gate trigger provided on any gate that also has a Pipe Timer attached to the same pipe.
</no_lore>

<chapter name="Requirements"/>
When selected, the connected actions will fire on a fixed cadence — once every 15 seconds (300 game ticks). The trigger is active for the single tick on which the timer fires, then resets.

<chapter name="Stacking Timers"/>
The 5, 10 and 15 second timers are independent triggers on the same Pipe Timer plug — pick whichever cadence fits the action you want to drive, or wire several at once for compound schedules.
